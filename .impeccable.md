### Design Principles

1. Communicate credibility fast: front-load identity, years of experience, and strongest proof points above the fold.
2. Motion with purpose: every animation should clarify hierarchy or tell a story, never decorative noise.
3. Dark UI with disciplined contrast: maintain comfortable readability and WCAG 2.1 AA compliance in text and controls.
4. Show evidence, not claims: skills, certificates, and projects should be measurable and scannable.
5. Keep interaction smooth and resilient: responsive layout, reduced-motion accommodation, and performant transitions.
6. Keep profile data truthful and current: prefer LinkedIn-backed facts and avoid invented credentials or inflated claims.
